Privacy Policy

Back Bar — last updated 25 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Myatt's Fields Limited ("Myatt's Fields", "we", "us"), a company registered in England and Wales, handles data accessed via Back Bar (the "Application"), an internal operations tool we operate for our own use.

1. Scope

Back Bar is a private, single-tenant application. It is not offered to third parties. Our QuickBooks integration accesses only Myatt's Fields's own QuickBooks Online company file.

2. Data accessed

Through the Intuit Developer API, Back Bar may access the following categories of data from our own QuickBooks company file:

  • Chart of accounts, transactions, invoices, bills, and payments
  • Customer and supplier records held in QuickBooks
  • Product, service, and pricing information
  • Tax codes and accounting metadata

We do not access QuickBooks data belonging to any other person or company.

3. How data is used

QuickBooks data is used solely to support Myatt's Fields's own accounting, reporting, and operational workflows — for example, reconciling sales from our Shopify store with QuickBooks records, or generating internal management reports. Data is never used for advertising, sold, or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Hosting and storage

Back Bar runs on infrastructure operated by Vercel Inc. and Railway Corp. Both providers offer encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. No copies of QuickBooks data are stored outside this infrastructure or shared with other systems beyond what is necessary to operate Back Bar.

5. Retention

Accounting data accessed via the QuickBooks integration is retained for up to six years from the end of the accounting period to which it relates, in line with HMRC record-keeping requirements. Operational logs and metadata are retained only as long as needed for the integration to function and are then deleted.

6. Your rights

Because Back Bar is operated solely for the internal use of Myatt's Fields, this policy does not generally engage data subject rights of third parties. Where any personal data of identifiable individuals (for example, customer or supplier contacts held in our QuickBooks) is processed, we comply with our obligations under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018 as data controller.

7. Revoking access

The QuickBooks connection can be revoked at any time from the Apps menu inside QuickBooks Online. Revocation immediately ends Back Bar's API access.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact
